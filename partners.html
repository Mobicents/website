Rancore Technologies

Rancore Technologies

 Headquartered in Navi Mumbai, India, Rancore Technologies a leading technology R&D firm focused on 4G telecom networks and services. Rancore's product line ranges from LTE and WiMAX Base Stations to the LTE Evolved Packet Core (EPC), the IP Multimedia Subsystem (3GPP IMS), Charging solutions (post-paid and pre-paid) to its own Service Delivery Platform (SDP). The SDP product line is based on the Mobicents JAIN SLEE platform, JBoss Enterprise Application Platform and Red Hat Enterprise Linux. The company has approximately 350 engineers, which consist of core 4G product designers, systems QA engineers, 4G telco subject matter experts, OSS/BSS experts and business analysts.

Mobicents Partner Certification Program

The Mobicents Partner Program aims to establish a trust relationship between the Mobicents core team, the ecosystem of Mobicents experts and the end customers around the world.

Certified Mobicents Partners are active contributors to the Mobicents community and have demonstrated ability to design and build innovative, robust and scalable communication applications based on the most popular Open Source Communications Platform.

Want to Join the Program?
If you are an Independent Software Vendor insterested in becoming a Certified Mobicents Partner, start by participating in the community forums. The core Mobicents team continuously monitors participation and periodically invites active contributors to apply for the Partner Program.
