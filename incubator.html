Introduction


Mobicents platform incubator is the place, where all the non-mature projects grow. Variety of the projects,subprojects,applications or protocols need a place, where can be grown to fully certified - so called: "adult project. Once the incubator babies are mature enough, they become part of already matured projects or become a stable project itself. Each incubator project comes with a short description about its purpose and also with roadmap defining milestones. With growing up, more info will come, so keep an eye on your favourites"
